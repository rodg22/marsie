ABOUT US

HISTORY

Three former university colleagues from Argentina reunite in 2019, after almost 15 years, to create Marsie. An American-based company born out of an entrepreneurial and investigative spirit, responding to market opportunities for nearshore software development expertise, having three main goals in mind:

Technical excellence as an ongoing endeavor, shown in our engineering skills, software quality, mindset, and practices.

Clear and simple rules, which stem from experience and are reflected in our strategy and policies.

Commitment, constant engagement to both our values and our people.

MISSION

Our purpose is to make things happen by listening to customers and providing effective software development solutions for their ideas and needs.

Every project we staff and participate in is driven to success by our team of highly skilled professionals.

DNA

We are passionate about creativity and software research, and are always pursuing innovative projects with teams eager to take on even bigger challenges.

We praise and honor the effort and talent of everyone who works together with us, providing equality, inclusion, growth, and personal development opportunities within a comfortable work environment.
